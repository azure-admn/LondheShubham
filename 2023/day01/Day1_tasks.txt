What is DevOps

DevOps is a methodology which creates bridge between different infrastructure for Integration & automation among various tools with scalability.

What is Automation, Scaling, Infrastructure

Automation is a process of DevOps thorugh which we can accomplish tasks automatically by using scripts , methods .

Why DevOps is Important, etc 

DevOps is Important & required for these reasons : 

1. creates bridge between development & operation teams .
2. for better avaliablity,scalable,secure & maintainable of any large scale system .
3. for automating manual processes in a mapped way .
4. To deliver any distributed system faster .
